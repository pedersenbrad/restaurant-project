The Taste of San Luis is a small family owned restaurant located in the wonderful town of Osceola Wisconsin. we bring you all your favorite flavors from the wonderful Mexican state of San Luis Potosi. All of our food is cooked with traditional methods and homemade warm tortillas and flavorful sauces. Come vist us and have a wonderful meal with your friends and family, and check out our rooftop seating with views of the wonderful St. Croix River valley.
